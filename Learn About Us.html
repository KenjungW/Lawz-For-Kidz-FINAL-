Hello! My name is Ken and as of September 2025, I am attending my sophomore year in Thailand. This law website acts not only as a school project for my sophomore personal project, but to express my true passion and interest for law and inspire others to have a similar interest. Lawz for Kidz acts as an informative website to inform kids about important legal landmark decisions in a simple yet concise manner, featuring cases from both the United States and United Kingdom with varying topics, ranging from free speech to pension rights, ensuring that young minds get a wide exposure into the legal world.
